Conditions Of Use

Academic Context: E-library resources, including data files, software, and media, are typically allowed for use within the academic context of research, education, and study. This means they should be utilized primarily for scholarly purposes.

Non-Commercial Use: Commercial use of e-library resources is prohibited. These resources are intended for educational and non-profit purposes, not for profit-making ventures.

Affiliation Restrictions: Users are generally not permitted to use e-library resources for organizations or companies outside the institution (such as a university or research center) to which they are associated.

License Agreements: E-libraries often have license agreements with publishers or content providers. Users must adhere to the terms specified in these agreements. These terms may cover aspects like access rights, usage limits, and restrictions.
