Privacy Notice

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), applicable in the European Union, mandates that organizations provide a clear and concise privacy notice. Your privacy notice should be easily accessible and written in plain language. It must explain:

Categories of Personal Data: Describe the types of personal data you collect (e.g., names, email addresses, browsing history). Legal Basis: Specify why you collect this data (your legal basis under GDPR). Data Processing: Clarify how you process the data (whether you’re the data controller or processor). Purpose: State the purpose of data collection (e.g., for marketing, account management).

Retention Period: Indicate how long you’ll keep the data and how you’ll dispose of it. Opt-Out Option: Provide users with the ability to opt out of data processing.
